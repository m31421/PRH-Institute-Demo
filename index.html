How it Works

Starting your career as a Phlebotomy Technician is possible with a program that sets you up for success.

Admission Requirements

Enrollment requires a completed agreement, $150 non-refundable fee, GED or high school diploma, passing entrance exam, government ID, Social Security card, Hep B vaccination proof, and both a background check and drug screen.

Online, Hybrid, or In-Person Learning

Choose the format that fits your schedule and learning style while still meeting all course requirements.

Practical, Career-Aligned Content

Train with professionals and develop skills taught through a mix of classroom learning and hands-on lab experience to prepare you for entry-level healthcare roles.

PruittHealth Institute is aligned with NHA for national certification testing and is an authorized testing site.

PruittHealth Institute is authorized by the Georgia Nonpublic Postsecondary Education Commission to operate the Phlebotomy Technician program.
